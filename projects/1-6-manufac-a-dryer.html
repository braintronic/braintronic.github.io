Project Description

Braintronic successfully designed and manufactured brand-new industrial air dryers to meet the growing demand for reliable and energy-efficient systems in the tea industry. Each dryer was fully engineered in-house, ensuring durability, dust protection, and seamless integration into different factory environments.

One of our key projects was the supply and installation of a new air dryer at Nandana Tea Factory, where our system delivered consistent performance, reduced moisture problems, and supported uninterrupted production.

The Challenge in Installation

The process of manufacturing and installing new dryers came with several challenges:

Heat & Dust Conditions – Factory floors operated in high-temperature, dusty environments, requiring sealed enclosures and enhanced cooling.

Factory-Specific Layouts – Each site had unique space and power conditions, so every dryer was custom-built for compatibility.

Durability Requirements – Continuous industrial use demanded long-lasting materials and components capable of withstanding stress.
Our engineering team overcame these challenges by using precision laser-cut chassis, custom airflow designs, and industrial-grade components.

“The new air dryer supplied by Braintronic to Nandana Tea Factory has truly upgraded our production. It runs reliably, reduced downtime, and most importantly, helped us cut energy costs. We are very satisfied with the performance and service.”

— Factory Engineer, Nandana Tea Factory

The Final View of Project

The manufacturing and installation of brand-new air dryers by Braintronic provided tea factories with:

  • Durable and reliable systems built for heavy-duty operation
  • Custom designs to suit factory-specific needs
  • Stable, energy-efficient performance
  • Quick commissioning and after-sales support

Every air dryer was powered, tested, and commissioned before handover, ensuring seamless operation.
