Air Dryers Repairing

Moisture Control Solutions for Industry

In tea processing, dry air isn’t a luxury — it’s a necessity. From protecting pneumatic tools to preserving leaf quality in automated lines, air dryers play a silent but essential role. At Braintronic, we provide expert maintenance and repair services for industrial air dryers, ensuring your compressed air systems stay dry, clean, and reliable all year round.

Why Tea Factories Need Reliable Air Dryers

The high humidity levels and fine tea dust inside Sri Lankan tea factories can wreak havoc on air-powered systems. Moisture in compressed air can lead to:

  • Corrosion in pneumatic tools
  • Product contamination
  • Inconsistent performance of packaging and sorting systems

That’s why keeping your air dryers in peak condition isn’t optional — it’s mission-critical. Braintronic offers full-service support for a wide range of air dryer technologies used across the tea industry.

Types of Air Dryers We Service

  • Refrigerated Air Dryers
    • Common in mid-size factories
    • Removes water vapor via cooling coils
    • Issues: Refrigerant leaks, clogged heat exchangers, control faults
    • Services: Gas refill, coil cleaning, thermostat replacement, PCB repairs
  • Desiccant (Adsorption) Air Dryers
    • Used in high-precision or moisture-sensitive operations
    • Removes moisture using silica gel or activated alumina
    • Issues: Media saturation, valve sticking, purge air loss
    • Services: Desiccant replacement, valve overhaul, PLC diagnostics
  • Membrane Air Dryers
    • Used in smaller or isolated applications
    • Removes water vapor using selective permeable membranes
    • Issues: Membrane clogging, low flow rate, external leaks
    • Services: Membrane cartridge replacement, pressure balancing
