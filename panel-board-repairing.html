Panel Board Repairing

Precision Repairs. Safer Systems. Smarter Performance.

Electrical and automation panel boards are the command centers of your industrial systems — from motors and compressors to entire production lines. When something goes wrong, even a small fault can lead to significant downtime, safety hazards, or production delays.

At Braintronic, we specialize in the repair, retrofitting, servicing, and performance testing of all types of industrial and commercial panel boards used across Sri Lanka. Whether you're dealing with a sudden failure, outdated components, or recurring electrical issues, our team ensures your systems are restored safely and efficiently.

Types of Panels We Service & Repair

  • Motor Control Centers (MCCs)
  • Power Distribution Boards (PDBs)
  • PLC Control Panels
  • VFD Panels
  • Star-Delta & DOL Starter Panels
  • Air Compressor & Dryer Control Panels
  • Boiler, Furnace, and Tea Drying System Panels
  • Custom-Built Panels (Local & Imported)

Our Panel Repair & Service Workflow

  1. Detailed Inspection & Diagnostics
    • Wiring integrity
    • Breakers, relays, contactors
    • Terminal blocks and busbars
    • Overload protection systems
    • Cooling fans, timers, and sensors
  2. Fault Detection & Root Cause Analysis
    • We identify hidden electrical faults, overheating points, damaged circuits, or automation misconfigurations.
  3. Component Replacement or Repair
    • Using OEM-grade or equivalent components, we replace or repair:
    • Burnt or corroded wires
    • Faulty relays, fuses, MCBs, MCCBs
    • Damaged HMI, timers, indicators, and selector switches
    • VFD modules, SMPS units, or PLC I/O cards
  4. Panel Retrofitting & Upgrades
    • We upgrade outdated panels to meet modern standards with:
    • New VFDs or energy-efficient starters
    • Safety interlocks and surge protectors
    • Improved layout for better access and cooling
    • Smart monitoring or SCADA integration (on request)
  5. Testing, Reporting, and Safety Assurance
    • All panels are tested under load or simulation before handing over. You receive a detailed service report, including photos, replaced parts, and any recommendations for preventive measures.
