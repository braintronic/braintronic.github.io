Tea Color Sorter Repairing Services

Expert Repair, Calibration & Optimization

Tea color sorters are vital to maintaining quality and consistency in modern tea processing facilities. At Braintronic, we specialize in the repair, calibration, and optimization of tea color sorting machines, ensuring your production line delivers high-grade output with maximum efficiency and minimal waste.

Why Precision Matters

Color sorters play a critical role in removing discolored, immature, or foreign tea particles, directly affecting the final product's market value and quality. Any malfunction or misalignment can lead to production losses, poor grading, or equipment damage — that's where our expertise comes in.

Our Repair & Servicing Approach

  • ✓ Detailed Diagnosis: Full diagnostic check — from sensor alignment and ejector testing to software/system health scans.
  • ✓ Component-Level Repair: We service and replace key components such as optical sensors, lenses, ejector valves, solenoids, vibrator feeders, conveyors, mainboard, control panels, air compressors, and filters.
  • ✓ Software Calibration & Tuning: Precise tuning of image recognition algorithms and color parameters for accurate sorting and reduced rejection of good material.
  • ✓ Preventive Maintenance: Scheduled plans to clean optical systems, replace filters, inspect electrical connections, and ensure consistent airflow and timing.
  • ✓ On-Site & Workshop Repairs: Emergency on-site service or full overhaul in our workshop for all major brands and models.
