VFD Installation

Smarter Compressor Control for Energy Savings & System Longevity

At Braintronic, we help tea factories move from fixed-speed inefficiency to intelligent, demand-driven performance through Variable Frequency Drive (VFD) installations on air compressors. VFDs offer a modern solution to one of the industry's most overlooked costs: excessive energy consumption in compressed air systems.

Why VFDs Matter in the Tea Industry

Tea factories don’t operate at a constant load — demand for compressed air varies throughout the day, during different processing stages. Traditional compressors either run at full speed or stop entirely, wasting energy, increasing wear, and leading to frequent maintenance.
With VFDs, compressor speed is automatically adjusted to match real-time air demand — giving factories:

  • Up to 35% energy savings
  • Extended compressor lifespan
  • Quieter operation
  • Reduced mechanical stress and heat generation

What is a VFD?

A Variable Frequency Drive (also called an Inverter Drive) controls the speed of an electric motor by varying the frequency and voltage of the power supplied. When installed on a compressor, a VFD enables smooth ramp-up, optimized speed control, and reduced energy spikes during startup.

Applicable Compressor Types

  • Rotary Screw Compressors – Ideal for VFD retrofits
  • Reciprocating Compressors – Moderate efficiency gain
  • Multi-Unit Systems – Load balancing via VFD sequencing

Benefits for Tea Factory Operations

  • Energy Efficiency – Cut unnecessary power costs in part-load conditions
  • Lower Maintenance – Reduce start/stop stress on motors and mechanical components
  • Extended Uptime – Fewer breakdowns due to thermal and mechanical stress
  • Process Optimization – Better air pressure stability for critical tea processing stages
