Rice Color Sorter Repairing

Precision Maintenance for High-Performance Grain Sorting

In modern rice mills, color sorters are mission-critical machines that ensure grain purity, reduce wastage, and uphold export-quality standards. At Braintronic, we offer specialized repairing and maintenance services for rice color sorters, delivering fast, reliable, and factory-grade solutions that keep your production lines running smoothly.

The Role of Color Sorters in Rice Mills

Rice color sorters use advanced optical sensors and AI algorithms to identify and remove defective grains, discolored rice, stones, and foreign materials. With Sri Lanka's growing rice exports and consumer demand for clean, high-quality rice, these machines are now essential at every commercial-scale mill.
Even a few minutes of downtime can lead to rejected batches, delivery delays, and production losses. That’s why Braintronic’s technical team ensures your sorter stays in optimal condition – always.

    • Solenoid and pneumatic valve testing

    • Realignment for fast and accurate rejection

    • Internal cooling fans, air filters, and temperature sensors

    • Vibrator feeder and chute servicing
    • Conveyor belt and hopper alignment
