Compressor Repairing

Expert Compressor Service for Industry

At Braintronic, we specialize in repairing, servicing, and maintaining industrial air compressors used across the Sri Lankan tea industry. From powering pneumatic leaf handling systems to driving automated packaging lines, compressors are the heartbeat of tea processing — and we’re here to keep them running at peak performance.

Why Tea Factories Trust Braintronic

Tea factories operate under harsh environmental conditions: fine dust, high humidity, long duty cycles — all of which can wear down even the toughest compressor systems. With years of hands-on experience inside real tea production floors, Braintronic offers precision-driven service solutions that ensure zero downtime and maximum energy efficiency.

Compressor Types We Repair & Service

  • Reciprocating (Piston) Compressors
    • Ideal for small to mid-scale operations
    • Common issues: valve damage, carbon buildup, piston wear
    • Services: Ring replacement, crankshaft repair, valve overhaul
  • Rotary Screw Compressors
    • Used for continuous-duty applications
    • Common issues: oil carryover, airend wear, control faults
    • Services: Airend rebuilding, bearing replacement, panel diagnostics
  • Scroll Compressors
    • Typically found in climate control or vacuum systems
    • Common issues: scroll element damage, motor failure
    • Services: Scroll chamber inspection, noise reduction, motor rewinding
  • Centrifugal Compressors (for large estates)
    • Central air solutions in high-volume facilities
    • Common issues: impeller wear, vibration, seal failure
    • Services: Dynamic balancing, impeller alignment, bearing replacement
